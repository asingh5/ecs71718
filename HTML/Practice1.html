Mahatma Gandhi

Mr. Peace

Mahatma Gandhi was a man who fought for and won India's independence when India was under the rule of Great Britain.
He was a man who stressed to be peaceful and harmless while still standing up for what's right.
He is an inspiration for many activists after him, such as Martin Luther King Jr.

  • The famous Salt March of 1914. Gandhi walked 241 miles with thousands of other Indians to protest the heavy salt tax, and to show the legitimacy of India's quest for independence.
  • The Quit India Movement in 1942 had Britain realize that it was impossible to rule India. His movement allowed 100,000 political prisoners to be released.
  • Gandhi is the leading figure of India's independence and is known worldwide for his achievement.
  • Gandhi was runner-up to Albert Einstein for Time's Person of the Century.
  • Gandhi led a non-violent tax revolt which successfully lowered the taxes of many farmers hit by famine and flood.
